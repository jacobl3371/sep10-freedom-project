Currently Existing Innovations:

My topic is about online services used for education, and how to facilitate the most compartmentalized, and pseudonymous access to your education, this being something that the world is lacking. The problem that I hope to solve with my project is that many educational services block access to their websites through Tor browser. Tor browser is the only tool that allows the user to access websites without them seeing the user's real IP address and subsequently, location by routing all traffic over the Tor network.
